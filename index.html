My name is Claire Joiner, and this is a web page that I designed all by myself! One of the many course requirements for my CSCI1100 lab was to design my own web page using Notepad C++. I learned many useful computer tips and tricks in the CSCI1100 lab, but I enjoyed learning how to use HTML the most. Check out the rest of my page to learn more about me!

I am currently a sophmore here at the University of Georgia, but I was a bulldog long before I got my acceptance letter to UGA. I attended high school at Emanuel County Institute in Twin City, Georgia where I was also a bulldog. Throughout my high school career I played tennis. I was also the president of our FCCLA club, and editor in chief of the school yearbook. In 2015 I graduated as salutatorian from ECI. Once I got to UGA it took me a while to choose a major, but after seeing what an amazing business school UGA has, I was able to narrow my search. Within the past semester I was accepted into UGA's Terry College of Business as a finance major.

Follow the link at the top of my page to learn more about the University of Georgia!
